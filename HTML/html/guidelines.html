Guidelines

Game Rules

The beginning of the game

The board has three rows of pegs: 5 short, 4 medium and 4 long. Press them into their rows from front to back. Thread all 16 pieces onto the 4 long pegs, alternating dark and light, 4 pieces per peg.

Objective

Be the first player to get at least 3 pieces of your color into the 5 indentations at the front of the board.

How to play

Piece colors do not matter!
Each turn consists of exactly two moves. You can move pieces of either color on your turn.
Sideways move
Move the top piece to an adjacent peg of the same height, as long as there is space. You cannot immediately undo the move you just made.
How to move forward?
Move the top piece forward to a lower peg or indentation, following the arrows on the board. This is only allowed if the piece is at the very top of its peg with no room above it.
Back in time!
You can undo your move! Be aware, you have only 3 undos per game. Not using undo's increases points you get or lose

Restrictions

  • Once a piece reaches the front indentations it cannot move again.
  • Backward moves are not allowed.
  • The last piece moved cannot be moved back to where it came from, even by the other player.

Winning

The first player to have 3 pieces of their color in the front indentations wins the game.

Installation Instructions

  1. Open your browser (You are here)
  2. Navigate to the main page (After you read all instructions)
  3. Download game and open it
  4. Enter as a guest or register account to gain more experience
  5. Have fun!

Technical Requirements

Computer
The game is only available for PC-users
JVM
You must have Java Virtual Machine installed on your PC
Internet connection
Non-essential requirement, but needed if you want to be part of leaderboard and save your progress as well as your settings
